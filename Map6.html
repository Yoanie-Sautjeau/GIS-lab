3D Map of Portland, US

I created this 3D map of Portland using a global projection scene by following the ESRI tutorial on Sharing 3D Content Using Scene Layer Packages. Through this tutorial, I learned how to create 3D scenes in ArcGIS and share them on ArcGIS Online. This map represents the buildings in Portland, located in the northwest region of the United States. It showcases the city's structures, trees, and topography, providing a detailed and immersive view of the urban landscape.
